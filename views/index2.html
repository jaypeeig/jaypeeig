Tayak Hill + Pandin Lake (Laguna) | Travel

Located in the City of San Pablo in the province of Laguna, Lake Pandin is one half of the Twin Lakes and also one of the Seven Lakes of San Pablo. Despite being smaller, Lake Pandin is deeper than Lake Yambo. It is also said that the waters of Lake Pandin is the most pristine of the Seven Lakes of San Pablo.
